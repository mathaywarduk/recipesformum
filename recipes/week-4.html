Salmon and Grain Salad

One of Kerry's staple lunches. Bulk it out with more salad if you're hungry!

Serves
2
Cooking time
20mins

Ingredients

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • 1 tsp paprika (or more or less if you prefer)
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp garlic granules or 1/2 garlic clove, minced
  • Olive oil
  • 1 packet Merchant Gourmet Glorious Grains
  • 1 small beetroot, diced
  • 1 courgette
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 100ml natural yoghurt
  • Salt and pepper

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C
  2. Lay salmon fillets skin-side down on a baking tray
  3. Drizzle over olive oil
  4. Sprinkle with paprika, oregano, garlic and a pinch each of salt and pepper
  5. Cook in the oven for 12-15 minutes
  6. While waiting, heat grains with a splash of water over a medium heat
  7. Use a hand peeler or mandolin to finely slice courgette lengthways and add to a mixing bowl
  8. Drizzle with 1/2 lemon juice and a pinch of salt and pepper
  9. Add diced beetroot and mix together
  10. Divide into two serving bowls
  11. Cover with heated grains
  12. Place salmon on top
  13. Serve
