Coconut Lentil Dahl

A great base for any meal or great for lunch with a flatbread. Make twice as much and reheat in the week..

Serves
2
Cooking time
35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil (vegetable or similar)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • Small piece of ginger, grated
  • 8 curry leaves
  • 1 red chilli (optional)
  • 1.5 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 2 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 50g red lentils
  • 400ml vegetable stock
  • 200ml can coconut milk
  • Large bag spinach
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Method

  1. Heat all in large pan over medium/high heat
  2. Add onion and cook for 5 minutes or until they begin to turn soft and brown
  3. Add garlic, ginger and chilli and cook for 2 minutes more
  4. Add curry leaves and spices. Cook and stir for 1 minute
  5. Add tomatoes, lentils and stock and stir
  6. Bring to the boil and then simmer for 18 minutes. Regularly checking and adding a splash of water if it begins to dry out
  7. Add spinach and coconut milk and simmer for 2 minutes more
  8. Season with salt and pepper
  9. Serve
