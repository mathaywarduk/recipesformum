Pasta with sausage and lentil ragu

Kerry made this for me last week and it's definitely the the best pasta ragu I've had at home... Dad might even like it!

Serves
2
Cooking time
45mins to 1hr

Ingredients

  • 150g conchiglie pasta
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 chipolata sausages, skinned
  • 1 clove garlic, sliced
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds, crushed
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 tbsp Beluga lentils, we use Merchant Gourmet
  • 3 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 150ml chicken stock
  • 25g parmesan, grated

Method

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan that has a lid
  2. Break bits of sausage into oil and fry until golden (breaking up with a wooden spoon as you go)
  3. Add garlic and cook for 2 minutes
  4. Add fennel seeds and oregano and fry for 1 minute
  5. Add lentils, tomatoes, purée and stock and bring to simmer
  6. Put the lid on and leave for 20 minutes
  7. Take the lid off and cook for at least another 20 minutes
  8. Cook the pasta as per packet instructions
  9. Drain the pasta and add to the ragu pan
  10. Mix and serve with grated parmesan on top
