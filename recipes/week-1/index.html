Sea Bass, Pilaf and Wilted Spinach

Serves
2
Cooking time
1hr 15mins

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup pearl barley
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red onion, diced
  • 1/2 bunch basil, chopped inc. stalks
  • 1 litre veg stock
  • 2 sea bass fillets
  • Half bag spinach
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 190Â°C
  2. Melt butter, with a splash of oil, in oven-proof pan over medium heat
  3. Add pearl barley, onion and 2 cloves garlic and cook down for 5 mins
  4. Add basil and stir for 30 seconds
  5. Add stock and pinch of salt and pepper
  6. Cover and cook in oven, set timer for 50 mins
  7. When timer goes, heat 1/2 tbsp oil in frying pan on med high heat
  8. Heat 1/2 tbsp oil in large saucepan on med heat
  9. Season sea bass and add to frying pan, skin-side down
  10. Add spinach and 1 clove garlic to saucepan and stir regularly
  11. After 5 mins, turn sea bass over and turn heat off
  12. Take pilaf out of oven, season and add lemon juice. Stir.
  13. Serve
