Courgette and Mozzarella Salad

A quick and easy lunchtime salad that's a bit different. Could easily turn this into a dinner by switching the mozzarella for some grilled fish.

Serves
2
Cooking time
10mins

Ingredients

  • 2 courgettes
  • Ball of mozzarella
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • Sunflower (or other) seeds to serve
  • Extra virgin olive oil to serve

For the dressing

  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of ground black pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Pinch chilli flakes (optional)
  • Small handful basil or mint leaves
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Method

  1. Combine dressing ingredients in a large mixing bowl
  2. Add sliced tomatoes to the bowl
  3. Use a hand peeler or mandolin to finely slice courgette lengthways and add to bowl
  4. Mix together and add to plates for serving
  5. Tear mozzarella ball into bitesize pieces and dot around top of salad
  6. Drizzle a little olive oil over mozzarella
  7. Scatter seeds on top
  8. Eat
