Tuna Burger with Sweet Potato Fries

One of my favourites. Leave the bun out if you like and save 164 calories and 33.8g of carbs.

Serves
2
Cooking time
1hr 15mins (includes 30 mins soaking time)

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sized sweet potatoes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 2 tuna steaks
  • 2 brioche buns
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/2 large tomato, diced
  • 1/2 large tomato, sliced
  • Salt and pepper

Method

  1. Peel and slice sweet potatoes into fries and soak in a bowl of cold water for 30 minutes
  2. Preheat oven to 220°C
  3. Add a tiny bit of oil to a backing tray and rub around, making sure you get to the edges. Alternatively, use a oil spray
  4. Drain the sweet potato fries and damp dry with kitchen roll
  5. Add sweet potato fries to a bowl, drizzle with 1 tbsp of olive oil and toss to evenly coat
  6. Sprinkle over the cornflour, a pinch each of the paprika and pepper
  7. Toss to combine, ensuring the cornflour soaks into the oil
  8. Crush the garlic cloves with the back of a knife
  9. Spread the sweet potato fries onto your baking tray and place the garlic cloves in and around them
  10. Cook in the oven for 25-30 minutes, flipping and spacing out in between
  11. While they are cooking, on a plate, drizzle remaining olive oil over tuna steaks and sprinkle with salt, pepper and the rest of the paprika
  12. Use your hands to mix spread the oil over the tuna
  13. Wash your hands!
  14. Slice the brioche buns in half
  15. In a bowl, crush the avocado with the back of a fork before adding the diced tomato and a pinch each of salt and pepper
  16. Mix
  17. Remove sweet potato fries from the oven and season with salt
  18. Let them rest while you cook the tuna
  19. Heat a frying pan over a high heat and get a clean plate ready on the side
  20. When it's hot add the tuna steaks and cook for 1-2 minutes each side
  21. Leave the tuna steaks to rest on the clean plate and use the same pan to lightly toast the inside of the brioche buns
  22. Serve by spreading the guacamole over the base of the bun, placing the tuna steak on top with a slice or two of tomato for good measure
